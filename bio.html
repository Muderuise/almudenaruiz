Almudena Ruiz is a young and emerging visual artist specialized in 3D art. Her last participation was in the contemporary art fair in Madrid JUSTMad (2020). The artist creates scenarios from her own imagination where she introduces herself as a voyeur and arranges the scene that she wants to reveal to the public. Her work aims to make the viewer question the nature of the image represented, through the play of the real and the fictitious. Mainly through inhospitable landscapes where silence and the feeling of Unheimlich reign: the valley between the familiar and the strange.
